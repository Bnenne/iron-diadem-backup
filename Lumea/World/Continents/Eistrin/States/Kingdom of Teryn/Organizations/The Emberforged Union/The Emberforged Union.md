# Summary
The Emberforged are a loose network of enchanters, blacksmiths, and craftsfolk who trade goods without royal tax or noble oversight. They advocate for fair trade and mutual support, not revolution, but independence from exploitation.
# Methods
Economic subversion, strikes, and collective enchantments to disrupt royal shipments.  
# Symbol
A hammer burning with inner fire.  
# Reputation
Idealistic dreamers, or organized criminals, depending on who you ask.
