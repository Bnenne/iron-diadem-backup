# Summary
A secretive group of mages, scholars, and warlocks obsessed with uncovering lost or suppressed magical lore, especially relics from before the divine wars. They believe power should belong to those with knowledge, not birthright.
# Methods
Research, infiltration, and quiet manipulation. They sell information to fund their work, but no one knows their true goal.
# Symbol
An open eye behind a half-drawn curtain.
# Reputation
Dangerous intellectuals; tolerated but feared.
