**Last entry, the night before she was killed**
> 
> 
> The kids asked me today what Aeloria was like. I told them about the gardens. I didn't tell them about the slums. I didn't tell them about being hungry. I didn't tell them about my mother leaving.
> 
> I want them to know beauty. I want them to know safety. I want them to have the childhood I didn't.
> 
> If something happens to me, I hope nothing happens to me, but if it does... Matt will take care of them. He's better at this than he thinks. He's better than he knows.
> 
> And maybe... maybe that's enough. Maybe that's my legacy. Three kids who get to grow up unafraid.
>

> I fear I may die soon, if so I want Laena to get my chipped dagger, my first weapon, when she's old enough
> 
> I want Matt to have the bracelet I stole as a child
