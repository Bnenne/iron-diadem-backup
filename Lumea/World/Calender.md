# Months
- Firstthaw - Early Spring - 30 days
- Rainrun - Spring rains - 30 days
- Blossomtide - Late Spring - 30 days
- Highsun - Early Summer - 30 days
- Goldgrain - Mid Summer - 30 days
- Harvestmoon - Late Summer - 30 days
- Bounty's End - Harvest Festival - 5 days (6 on leap year)
- Sundew - Early Autumn - 30 days
- Redleaf - Mid Autumn - 30 days
- Emberfall - Early Winter - 15 days
- Frostfall - Early Winter - 15 days
- Deepwinter  - Mid Winter - 30 days
- Longnight - Dead of Winter - 30 days
- Thawing  - Late Winter - 30 days
# Weekdays
Weeks are 6 days long and do not overflow (meaning they week resets back to the beginning each month)

# Eras
- The Primordial Age - Year 0
- The Age of Rising Gods - Year 1-800
- The War of the Gods - Year 800-1200
- Post-War Reconstruction - Year 1200-1400
- The Fracturing - Year 1400-1600
- Modern Era - Year 1600-Current
# Date Formats
- **Era Format**
	- (Month) (Day), (Era Year) (Era Abbr.)
		- Ex. Firstthaw 25, 248 ME 
- **Creation Format**
	- (Month) (Day), (Year since creation) AC
		- Ex. Firstthaw 25, 1847 AC
- **Full Formal**
	- (Month) (Day), (Era Year) (Era Abbr.) ((Year since creation) AC)
		- Ex. Firstthaw 25, 248 ME (1847 AC)
