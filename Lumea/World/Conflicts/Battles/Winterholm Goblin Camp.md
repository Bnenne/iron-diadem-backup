# Terrain
- Under a small cliff
- Large log walls surrounding the camp with an open gate
- 2 Tall lookout towers
- 6 small tents
- 1 large tent in the back center
- Bonfire in the center
# The Fight
- Goblin Boss stationed on one tower, Goblin Hexer stationed on the other
- 3 Goblin Warriors around the bonfire
- 4 Goblin Minions in the tents
# Lore
The Goblins were paid by the [[King Vaerin Albretcht|King]] to not just destroy [[Winterholm]] but to kill [[Matt Stonebridge]]. [[High Priest Marcion Vale]] received a prophecy of a soldier (Matt Stonebridge) who would rise up against the King.
- The goblins have gear they shouldn't
	- Military tents
	- Quality weapons
	- A chest of more gold than they should be able to acquire
- A [[Military Ledger|ledger]] recounting previous interactions with the King and [[Atlas Stahl]]
