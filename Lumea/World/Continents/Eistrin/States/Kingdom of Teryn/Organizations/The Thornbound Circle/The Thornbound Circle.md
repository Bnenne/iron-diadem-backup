# Summary
The Thornbound Circle believes the forest itself is alive, a divine organism wounded by human greed and royal exploitation. They see the [[King Vaerin Albretcht|King]]’s logging and magical extraction as acts of desecration and have vowed to defend the “Heartwood” by any means necessary.
# Methods
Guerrilla tactics, sabotage of trade routes, assassinations of royal envoys.  
# Symbol
A wreath of thorns wrapped around a bleeding branch.  
# Reputation
To the West, heroes. To the Crown, terrorists.
