A druidic order older than [[Kingdom of Teryn|Teryn]] itself. They focus on preserving the balance of nature and guiding settlements toward sustainable practices. While sympathetic to [[The Thornbound Circle]], they condemn their violence.
# Methods
Mediation, protective wards, diplomacy with magical beings.
