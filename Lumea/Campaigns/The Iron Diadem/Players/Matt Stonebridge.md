# Biography !hide
A young Paladin from [[Winterholm]] and Veteran who fought in the [[The Ochre War]] as a member of [[The People's Legion]]. Four months after returning home from the war his village was attacked by Goblins, his family was killed and he fled. He now travels Teryn searching for anywhere he can get a good drink.
